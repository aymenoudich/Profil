About Chabab Nahda Association

Hello

The Renaissance Youth Association Chabab Nahda in 2011 with coach Sekou Bezy

This Association specializes in football for seniors aged 18 and over

From 2011 to 2024 they were present in all categories but in 2025 coach Sekou bezy decided that he would remain in the Association in the senior category only

We have training sesseion for players three times a week

Training sessions will be held in local stadiums, such as the Al-Wahda Group in the Hay Nahda in Rabat local stadium, or the Nahda 1 local stadium

We also have a match every week

We participate in annual championships in the Rabat-Salé-Kenitre region

We also paricipate in the FUS Rabat Club Championship and the FAR Club Championship

We won many awards, including five FUS Rabat championships and two FAR Rabat championships
